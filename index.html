Our Story

From one roadside pot to
your doorstep — every day.

MamaPut started in 2011 with one firewood stove, one bench, and a pot of egusi that sold out before noon. Neighbours would shout — "Mama, put my food!" — and the name stuck.

Today we run three kitchens across Benin City with a dedicated delivery fleet. Order from your phone and our riders bring the same soul-food magic straight to you — no cold food, no compromise.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +

Fill in the order form in the Order section above, or message us on WhatsApp with your meal, name, address and preferred delivery time. Our team will confirm and dispatch right away.

How long does delivery take? +

Most deliveries arrive within 25–40 minutes of order confirmation. During peak hours (12pm–2pm and 6pm–8pm) allow up to 50 minutes. We always call ahead if there's any delay.

Can I customise my meal? +

Yes! Use the special notes field — less pepper, no onions, extra meat, specific combinations. We accommodate dietary preferences wherever possible.

What payment methods do you accept? +

Cash on delivery, bank transfer, and POS on delivery. For office and event packages we issue invoices for bank transfer. Account details are sent at order confirmation.

What if my order is wrong or arrives cold? +

Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or phone. If your order was incorrect or quality was unsatisfactory, we will resend or issue a full credit toward your next order — no questions asked.

Do you handle large event catering? +

Absolutely. We've catered for events from 50 to 800 guests — weddings, burials, office launches and corporate dinners. Contact us at least 5 days before the event for large bookings.

Are your ingredients fresh and natural? +

100%. We use fresh palm oil, real iru (locust beans), whole crayfish, and freshly sourced leaves and proteins every morning. No artificial flavourings, no preservatives.
